Description

Time goes by, competitors are developing their own coffee machines to attract coffee lovers with special types of coffee or special interesting features. It's time to enhance your coffee machine to compete with them!

In this stage, you should create something you would like to add to your coffee machine!

Objective

This stage will not be tested. Try to be creative, add something new to your coffee machine. It can be a new type of coffee, different kinds of milk, or different volumes of disposable cups.

Leave a comment with a link to the full source code and write what you added to the coffee machine! After that, check the appropriate option in the quiz.

Good luck!
